Gangtok

District Overview

The capital city known for its tourism, handicrafts, and growing IT sector. Hub of economic activities in Sikkim. Gangtok is the capital and largest city of the Indian state of Sikkim. It is also the headquarters of the East Sikkim district. Gangtok is located in the eastern Himalayan range at an elevation of 1,650 m (5,410 ft).

Key Industries

  • Tourism & Hospitality (45% of economy)
  • Handicrafts & Handloom
  • IT & Software Services
  • Pharmaceutical Manufacturing
  • Retail & Urban Services

Major Initiatives

  • Gangtok Smart City Project (₹500 crore investment)
  • IT Park Development
  • Tourism Infrastructure Enhancement
  • Handicraft Promotion Centers

Investment Opportunities

  • Eco-Tourism Development
  • IT & ITES Sector
  • Pharmaceutical Manufacturing
  • Hospitality Industry
  • Retail & Entertainment

Success Stories

Sikkim Manipal Institute of Technology has incubated over 25 startups in the last 5 years, creating a growing tech ecosystem in Gangtok. The city's pharmaceutical sector has attracted over ₹200 crore in investments since 2018.
