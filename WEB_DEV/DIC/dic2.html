Sikkim District Industries

Discover the economic potential and industrial opportunities across Sikkim's six vibrant districts.

Gangtok

The capital city known for its tourism, handicrafts, and growing IT sector. Hub of economic activities in Sikkim.

Key Industries

  • Tourism & Hospitality (45% of economy)
  • Handicrafts & Handloom
  • Information Technology
  • Education & Training

Government Schemes

Special subsidies for eco-tourism projects (up to ₹25 lakhs), IT startup incubation center with 2-year tax holiday.

Investment Potential

₹1,200 crore tourism projects underway. 25% capital subsidy for premium hotels.

Namchi

Sikkim's tea capital with sprawling plantations and spiritual tourism sites. Major spice producer.

Key Industries

  • Tea Plantations (200+ estates)
  • Spice Production (cardamom, ginger)
  • Religious Tourism
  • Floriculture

Government Schemes

30% subsidy on organic certification. ₹10 lakhs grant for tea processing units.

Investment Potential

500 acres available for spice parks. 15% export incentive for tea.

Gyalshing

Agricultural heartland with organic farms and dairy cooperatives. Emerging ecotourism destination.

Key Industries

  • Organic Farming (100% organic status)
  • Dairy Products
  • Apple Orchards
  • Eco-Tourism

Government Schemes

50% subsidy on cold storage. Free organic training programs.

Investment Potential

300+ hectares available for contract farming. Milk processing plant incentives.

Mangan

Northern district with hydropower projects and adventure tourism. Rich in minerals.

Key Industries

  • Hydropower (300MW capacity)
  • Adventure Tourism
  • Horticulture (kiwi, oranges)
  • Mineral Mining

Government Schemes

Tax holiday for power projects. 25% subsidy on ropeways.

Investment Potential

4 new hydropower projects tendered. Adventure resort land available.

Soreng

Newest district with bamboo forests and emerging rural tourism. Traditional crafts hub.

Key Industries

  • Bamboo Crafts
  • Rural Tourism
  • Poultry Farming
  • Apiculture

Government Schemes

40% subsidy on bamboo processing units. Homestay development grants.

Investment Potential

Bamboo industrial park planned. 200+ rural tourism sites identified.

Pakyong

Gateway district with international airport. Logistics and ginger farming center.

Key Industries

  • Aviation Services
  • Logistics & Warehousing
  • Ginger Farming
  • Food Processing

Government Schemes

30% subsidy on cold chains. Airport-linked industrial incentives.

Investment Potential

500-acre logistics park planned. 100% FDI allowed in aviation services.
